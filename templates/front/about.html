Our Story

Founded in 2020, Sample Store began with a simple vision: make high-quality products accessible to everyone. From a small shop in Phnom Penh, we’ve grown into a trusted online destination for electronics, fashion, and accessories.

We believe shopping should be easy, affordable, and enjoyable. That’s why we focus on curated collections, seamless service, and customer-first values.

Our Mission

To empower people with products that improve daily life, blending quality, style, and affordability.

Our Vision

To become Cambodia’s most loved e-commerce brand, delivering smiles to every home we serve.
